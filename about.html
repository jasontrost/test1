The Problem

Traditional betting has dominated for centuries, but it's fundamentally broken.

High Margins

Bookmakers extract 15-20% margins from every bet, significantly reducing customer returns and market efficiency.

Lack of Transparency

Odds are set arbitrarily. Customers never see the true probability of an outcome or understand the pricing.

Poor User Experience

Legacy platforms are slow, clunky, and disconnected from modern expectations of speed and usability.

Limited Liquidity

With centralized bookmakers, betting options are restricted and liquidity pools are artificially limited.

Inefficient Discovery

True market probability is hidden behind corporate pricing strategies rather than discovered through trading.

No Asset Class

Betting has no secondary market, no ability to trade positions, and no clear asset ownership model.

Our Story

From an ambitious startup to a global prediction market leader.

2008
Founded in London
Jason Trost and Hunter Morris founded Smarkets with a vision to revolutionize prediction markets through technology and transparency.
2010
Exchange Goes Live
The Smarkets exchange launches, introducing transparent, peer-to-peer prediction market trading to the world.
2015
Self-Management Model
Transitioning to self-managing teams with radical transparency and self-set salaries. We believe in trusting our people.
2020
£1B+ Annual Volume
A major milestone reached as Smarkets processes over £1 billion in annual trading volume on its platform.
2023
Launched SBK Sportsbook
Introducing SBK, our sportsbook brand, expanding our offerings beyond exchange-based prediction markets.
2025
AFC Bournemouth Partnership
SBK becomes the official training kit sponsor of AFC Bournemouth, bringing Smarkets to a new audience.
2026
SailGP Official Sports Trading Partner
Becoming the official sports trading partner for SailGP, expanding our presence in premium sports partnerships.
