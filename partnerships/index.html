Smarkets is proud to announce a multi-year partnership as the Official Sports Trading Partner of the Rolex SailGP Championship. Official Training Wear Sponsor — AFC Bournemouth

SBK, Smarkets' dedicated sportsbook brand, proudly sponsors AFC Bournemouth as their Official Training Wear Partner for the 2025/26 Premier League season. SBK branding appears on the front of all first-team training wear, connecting our brand with one of the Premier League's most exciting clubs.

Beyond kit sponsorship, SBK serves as the Presenting Partner of the AFC Bournemouth podcast, delivering premium digital content and maintaining a dedicated presence in the club's training gallery. Our Brands

The Exchange

Smarkets operates the peer-to-peer prediction market exchange, offering the sharpest prices and deepest liquidity in the industry. With billions of pounds in annual trading volume, our exchange provides a transparent, fair platform for trading on real-world outcomes.

Founded in 2008, Smarkets pioneered prediction market trading in Europe and continues to set the standard for exchange-based sports and event trading.

The Sportsbook

SBK is our sports-focused sportsbook brand, delivering an alternative to traditional betting operators. With no casino offerings and odds derived directly from our exchange liquidity, SBK provides transparent pricing backed by real market data.

Launched in 2023, SBK brings the innovation and fairness of prediction markets to traditional sports betting, ensuring customers receive competitive odds at all times.
