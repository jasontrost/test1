Why Smarkets

Profitable since 2017

We've been consistently profitable for multiple years — named "one of the most profitable per capita companies in Europe" by Corporate Rebels. Our exchange model generates strong unit economics without heavy capital requirements.

18 years of execution

Founded in 2008, we've outlasted competitors and survived every market cycle. That kind of longevity in a fast-moving industry isn't luck — it's a fundamentally sound model and a team that knows how to execute.

Two products, one engine

The Smarkets exchange serves institutional and sophisticated traders. SBK captures retail demand through a simpler sportsbook experience. Both run on the same proprietary technology — matching engine, market maker, and risk system — all built in-house.

Engineering-led

65% of our 100+ person team are engineers. We build everything ourselves — exchange infrastructure in Rust and C++, real-time frontends in React, and proprietary market-making algorithms. This gives us a compounding technical advantage.

Regulated in five jurisdictions

Licensed by the UK Gambling Commission, Malta Gaming Authority, and in the US (Indiana), Ireland, and Sweden. CFTC approval pending for broader US market access.

Capital efficient growth

Our exchange model doesn't require the heavy marketing spend that traditional sportsbooks burn through. We attract customers through better pricing, and our flywheel compounds from there.
