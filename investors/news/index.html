PARTNERSHIP February 2026

Smarkets becomes first Official Sports Trading Partner of SailGP (opens in new tab)

A 3-year exclusive partnership with the Rolex SailGP Championship, covering the UK, Ireland, and Sweden. The deal includes the Emirates Great Britain SailGP Team and the Portsmouth Grand Prix event. This is the first time a prediction market platform has partnered at this level with a global sporting league.

ANNUAL REPORT January 2026

FY 2024 Annual Report filed with Companies House (PDF, opens in new tab)

Smarkets Limited published its audited annual accounts for the year ended 31 December 2024. Revenue reached $31.4M with EBITDA of $10.2M and gross margins of 79%. The full report is available on the annual reports section.

BRAND August 2025

SBK launches AI-built ad campaign for Premier League season (opens in new tab)

SBK unveiled a new advertising campaign built with AI tools, marking a new approach to how sportsbook brands market to football audiences. The campaign supports SBK's continued growth in the UK retail sportsbook market.

PARTNERSHIP July 2025

SBK sponsors AFC Bournemouth training kit (opens in new tab)

SBK became the Official Training Wear Sponsor of AFC Bournemouth for the 2025/26 Premier League season, with branding on all first-team training wear. This extends Smarkets' sports partnership portfolio following the SailGP and DP World Tour relationships.
