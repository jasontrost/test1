London HQ

St. Katharine Docks — right next to Tower Bridge, on the Thames.

Our headquarters sits in one of London's most distinctive locations. St. Katharine Docks is a converted marina just east of the City, surrounded by water, boats, and some of the best lunch spots in Zone 1. The office itself is open-plan, built for collaboration — engineers sit with traders, product sits with operations. There are no corner offices.

Food at Smarkets

Daily meals are part of how we work — and how we connect.

We have a full kitchen and an in-house chef. Lunch is served every day for the whole team — it's when engineers talk to traders, product people swap ideas with ops, and new joiners get to know everyone. Food isn't a perk; it's infrastructure for collaboration.

Meet Alex — In-house Chef

What's your approach to feeding the team?

I try to cook food that brings people together. Different cuisines, seasonal ingredients, and always something for everyone. The kitchen is the heart of the office.

What's the most requested dish?

It changes, but the team always goes wild for fresh pasta day.

Malta

Our regulatory and operations hub in the Mediterranean.

Our Malta office handles regulatory compliance, customer operations, and supports our European licences under the Malta Gaming Authority. It's a smaller team with a tight-knit culture, working closely with London across time zones. The office overlooks the harbour — not a bad commute.
