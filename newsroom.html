About Smarkets

Smarkets is Europe's leading prediction market platform and exchange, founded in 2008 and headquartered in London with operations in Malta. The company operates two primary brands: Smarkets, the peer-to-peer exchange offering the sharpest odds in prediction market trading, and SBK, a sports-focused sportsbook featuring transparent, exchange-powered pricing.

With billions in annual trading volume and partnerships across world-class sporting events including the Rolex SailGP Championship and AFC Bournemouth, Smarkets is transforming how people trade on real-world outcomes. The platform is regulated in multiple jurisdictions and serves customers across the UK, Ireland, Sweden, and beyond.
