Company boilerplate

Smarkets is a prediction market founded in 2008 and headquartered in London. The company operates three products — the Smarkets prediction market, the SBK sportsbook, and Hanson, an institutional market maker — processing over $47B in lifetime traded volume across 5 licensed jurisdictions. Backed by Susquehanna Growth Equity, Passion Capital, and DTCP.

Executive bios

Jason Trost, CEO & Founder. Founded Smarkets in 2008 with the thesis that prediction market pricing could outperform traditional bookmaking. Previously worked in quantitative finance.

William Chambers, COO. Leads operations across product, compliance, and growth. Oversees Smarkets' expansion into new jurisdictions and markets.

Key facts

Founded: 2008 · HQ: London · Team: 100+ (65% engineers) · 2024 revenue: $31.4M · Profitable · Licensed in 5 jurisdictions
