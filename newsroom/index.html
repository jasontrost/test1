Executive bios

Jason Trost, CEO & Founder. Founded Smarkets in 2008 with the thesis that exchange-based pricing could outperform traditional bookmaking. Previously worked in quantitative finance.

William Chambers, COO. Leads operations across product, compliance, and growth. Oversees Smarkets' expansion into new jurisdictions and markets.

Company boilerplate

Smarkets is a prediction market exchange founded in 2008 and headquartered in London. The company operates three products — the Smarkets prediction market, the SBK sportsbook, and Hanson, an institutional market maker — processing over $47 billion in lifetime traded volume across five licensed jurisdictions. Backed by Susquehanna Growth Equity, Passion Capital, and DTCP.
