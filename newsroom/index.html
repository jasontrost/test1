Company boilerplate

Founded in 2008 and headquartered in London, Smarkets is a financial technology company operating a regulated prediction market for trading on the outcomes of major global events. The company runs three core products: the Smarkets exchange, the SBK consumer sportsbook, and a proprietary market-making business. Built on proprietary exchange technology and focused on transparent, market-driven pricing, the platform has processed more than $60B in lifetime traded volume across 5 licensed jurisdictions. Smarkets is backed by institutional investors including Susquehanna Growth Equity, Passion Capital, and DTCP.
