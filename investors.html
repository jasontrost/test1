The Opportunity

Market Size

Smarkets is positioned at the intersection of two high-growth markets: $80B in online sports betting and $90B in online financial trading. As fintech and betting converge, prediction markets represent an enormous TAM of $170B+.

Exchange Advantage

Our peer-to-peer exchange model captures transaction volume without taking principal risk. With no house edge, customers receive superior prices while Smarkets enjoys more sustainable, capital-efficient economics than traditional betting operators.

Regulatory Tailwinds

Prediction markets are gaining regulatory legitimacy globally. Recent CFTC approval of contract markets validates the asset class. Smarkets is licensed across multiple jurisdictions, positioned to lead as regulation matures worldwide.
