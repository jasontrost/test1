This page is for engineers, traders, and operators who think markets are fascinating. We're based in London and Malta.

What we're looking for

We hire improvement-minded people with a competitive spirit who take pride in their work. If you're passionate about building great products, thrive in a fast-paced environment, and want to win, this is the place.

You'll work at the intersection of finance, tech, and real-time systems. We celebrate wins, learn from failures, and genuinely like each other.

You'll thrive here if…

  • You want to do genuinely great work, not just keep busy
  • You like being part of a small, intentionally lean team where your work matters
  • You value complete internal transparency — we share financials daily
  • You want high autonomy paired with high expectations
  • You're comfortable setting your own salary based on the value you deliver
  • You enjoy shared ownership — everyone does customer support rotations

This probably isn't for you if…

  • You need detailed instructions and close supervision
  • You prefer large, structured organisations with clear hierarchies
  • You're looking for a place that prioritises comfort over performance
  • You think "that's not my job" when you see a problem
  • You'd rather optimise process than ship product
