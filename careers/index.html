What to expect

Our hiring process is simple, fast, and respectful of your time.

1

Apply

Submit your application through Ashby. We review every submission and aim to respond within a week. No cover letter required — your work speaks for itself.

2

Interview

A short intro call followed by a technical or role-specific interview. For engineering roles, this includes a practical coding exercise. We keep rounds to a minimum.

3

Decision

We move quickly. You'll hear back within days, not weeks. If it's a yes, we'll make an offer and talk you through compensation and benefits.

Our values

These aren't aspirational posters on a wall. They're how we actually hire, give feedback, and make decisions every day.

01

Give a Shit

Care about the organisation beyond your daily remit.

What this looks like

You notice a colleague struggling and offer help before being asked. You pick up trash from the office floor even though it's not your job. When something breaks, you stay focused until it's fixed — not because someone told you to, but because you care about the outcome.

02

Make Others Better

The best teams aren't solo stars — they make everyone around them better.

What this looks like

You make your code easier to read so the next person can move faster. You proactively review others' work. You share context, write things down, and help new joiners get up to speed. Collaboration isn't a buzzword — it's how we ship.

03

Be a Pro

High performance and continuous focused training. Set and maintain high standards.

What this looks like

You go beyond expectations. You pursue continuous learning and mastery of your craft. Precision, transparency, and a relentless focus on quality — the same standards that guide our markets guide how you work.

04

Bring the Energy

Positive environments enable better achievements. Energy drives collaboration.

What this looks like

You show up with intensity and drive. You motivate with enthusiasm and make people excited to work with you. Passion is contagious — and so is apathy. We choose passion.

05

Push to Win

Seek out challenges. That's how we grow and how we outperform.

What this looks like

You lean into challenges instead of avoiding them. You push to be the best and bring your team along for the journey. We compete against companies with 10x our headcount and 100x our marketing budget. We win by being sharper, faster, and more determined.

Working at Smarkets

An honest look at what it's like — so you can decide if it's for you.

You'll thrive here if…

  • You want to do genuinely great work, not just keep busy
  • You like being part of a small, intentionally lean team where your work matters
  • You value complete internal transparency — we share financials daily
  • You want high autonomy paired with high expectations
  • You're comfortable setting your own salary based on the value you deliver
  • You enjoy shared ownership — everyone does customer support rotations

This probably isn't for you if…

  • You need detailed instructions and close supervision
  • You prefer large, structured organisations with clear hierarchies
  • You're looking for a place that prioritises comfort over performance
  • You think "that's not my job" when you see a problem
  • You'd rather optimise process than ship product

How we play

Work is important. So is not working.

We eat together (daily free meals from our kitchen), we compete together (poker nights, sports events, gaming tournaments), and we travel together (team trips, off-sites, and the occasional questionable karaoke). Our culture isn't manufactured — it's what happens when you hire people who actually like each other.
