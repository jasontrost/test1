We hire engineers and operators in London and Malta to build products at the intersection of markets, software, and real-time systems.

What we're looking for

We hire improvement-minded people with a competitive spirit who take pride in their work. We look for people who care about building strong products, perform well in fast-paced environments, and want to work on ambitious technical and product challenges.

You'll work at the intersection of finance, tech, and real-time systems. We celebrate wins, learn from failures, and have fun together.

You'll thrive here if…

  • You want to do genuinely great work, not just keep busy
  • You like being part of a small, intentionally lean team where your work matters
  • You value a high level of internal transparency, including regular visibility into company performance metrics
  • You want high autonomy paired with high expectations
  • You enjoy working outside your lane when needed for the business

This probably isn't for you if…

  • You prefer large, structured organisations with strong hierarchies
  • You prefer highly structured environments with more process and predictability
  • You think "that's not my job" when you see a problem
  • You prefer process ownership over shipping and iteration
