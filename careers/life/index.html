Life at Smarkets

Our values, how we work, and why people stay.

Our values

These aren't aspirational posters on a wall. They're how we actually hire, give feedback, and make decisions every day.

01

Give a Shit

Care beyond your job description.

What this looks like

You spot a colleague stuck and help before they ask. When something breaks at 2am, you're already fixing it not because you're on-call, but because you care.

02

Make Others Better

The best teams elevate everyone.

What this looks like

You write code that's easy for the next person to understand. You review PRs thoughtfully. You share context, document decisions, and help new joiners ramp up fast. Collaboration isn't a buzzword, it's how we ship.

03

Be a Pro

High standards, continuous improvement.

What this looks like

You go beyond expectations. You read the research. You care about correctness, performance, and clarity. Precision matters, in code, in markets, in how we talk to customers.

04

Bring the Energy

Positive intensity drives performance.

What this looks like

You show up with focus and drive. You make people excited to work with you. You push through hard problems without dragging the room down. Passion is contagious and so is apathy. We choose passion.

05

Push to Win

Seek out challenges. That's how we grow.

What this looks like

You lean into hard problems instead of avoiding them. You want to be the best and bring your team along. We compete against companies with 10x our headcount and 100x our budget. We win by being sharper, faster, and more determined.

How we work

Bias to action

We value debate and challenge, but everything must be through the lens of the mission. Strong opinions, balanced with pragmatism and speed.

Customer obsession

We obsess about our product constantly. Everyone observes what customers want, talks to them and reads reviews.

In-person by default

3 days/week minimum in the office. We designed our space for focused work and collaboration. Remote tools bridge the gaps, but the core work happens together.

High-autonomy teams

Small teams, real ownership. You build it, you ship it, you own it.

Own the full stack

We build everything in-house — matching engine, market maker, risk system, frontends. No outsourced core. See our technology →

Competitive compensation

Benchmarked against top-tier tech companies and reviewed regularly. Equity participation so you share in the company's growth.

How we celebrate

Work is important. So is not working.

We work hard. We also know how to turn it off. We eat together every day, free lunch from our in-house chef. It's not a perk, it's how we connect. We talk about work, life, and occasionally argue about why we lost money that week.

Every quarter we host all-hands meetings, every month we host townhalls where teams present their wins. We celebrate major launches, recognise great work, and roast each other's bugs. Summer parties, Christmas dinners, and an internal hackathon round out the year.

We compete: poker nights, sports watch parties, gaming tournaments. We travel together for off-sites. We occasionally commit to questionable karaoke.

The work matters. So does not working. If you're going to spend 40+ hours a week with people, you should actually like them. We do.
